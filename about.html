About Sea breeze

Welcome to Sea Breeze Home Stay, a tranquil retreat nestled in the picturesque town of Malvan in Revandi. Our beautifully designed villa offers breathtaking views of the sea, making it the perfect escape for those seeking peace and relaxation.

Our homestay features four spacious rooms, each thoughtfully furnished to provide our guests with comfort and convenience. You can choose to book an individual room or the entire villa, ideal for families or groups of friends travelling together.

At Sea Breeze Home Stay, we believe every guest should have an unforgettable experience. That's why we offer a range of amenities, including sightseeing, boating, and water sports, to ensure that your stay with us is nothing short of exceptional.

Located just 7 kilometers from the famous Sindhudurg Fort in Malvan, and 35 kilometers from the exciting Zip Line, our homestay is the perfect base for exploring the many attractions in the area. For those who want to experience more, Goa is just 160 kilometers away.
